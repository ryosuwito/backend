What We Do

Dynamic Technology Lab Pte. Ltd. (DTL) designs technological innovations to intelligently analyze information to uncover irrationality in the seemingly rational financial markets. By adopting a diverse range of ideas, we pursue the goal of continuously generating high risk-adjusted investment returns for our clients.

Our Investment Approach

Our investment approaches are based on quantitative analyses which rely on mathematical models and number crunching to predict future security performances. The models built on historical data are used to measure the value of a security and identify potential trading opportunities, in an effort to identify potential trading opportunities. They are designed to utilize the best minds in the business and the fastest computers to both exploit inefficiencies and make market bets.

The methods we use provide us with unique advantages over traditional investors. Firstly, we use logic and historical facts, rather than emotional biases to drive decisions. Secondly, quantitative approach is consistent and repeatable because it uses a systematic investment methodology for security selection, and to enforce strong disciplines.
